Our History

About Us

Founded with a vision to lead the global transformation of urban infrastructure, AEINS Connect specializes in providing premium solutions that bridge sustainability and technology. Based in Kerala, India, we focus on creating AI-integrated, eco-friendly products that solve modern urban challenges. Our journey began with the AEINS Smart Bin, and we’ve since expanded into AI-powered apps, EV charging stations, and other groundbreaking innovations. Join us in our mission to shape a smarter, greener tomorrow.
